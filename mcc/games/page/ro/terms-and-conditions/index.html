Purchasing Your First Credits

Purchasing credits with Miniclip.com is easy. You will need to create a Miniclip Players account and be logged in to that account to purchase credits. Don't worry if you do not already have one, you will be prompted to create one when you place your order. If you experience any difficulties when purchasing credits please email us at: support@miniclip.com.

Here are the steps that you need to follow to purchase credits:

  1. Browse the "shop" in any relevant Miniclip game

    First you will need to be playing a Miniclip game in which you can use credits to purchase supplemental products or items. A clickable 'Shop' button will be available on the game-play page where you can browse and purchase products with your credits. After clicking on the 'Shop' page you will be able to click on an option to purchase credits.

  2. Login and/or Create a Miniclip players account

    In order to purchase any credits, you must have a Miniclip Players account and be logged in to this account. You will be prompted to login or to create a Miniclip Players account if you have not already done so. Please follow the on-screen instructions to create a Miniclip Players account – creation of any account should occur almost instantly. You will receive an email to the email address you submitted confirming the creation of your account and containing your password.

  3. Select a bundle of credits and proceed to checkout

    You will be presented with credit bundles of varying prices to choose from. Please select a bundle that you wish to purchase, review this selection and, if you are happy, please proceed to check out. You will automatically be redirected to a third party payment provider website.

  4. Provide payment information

    Now let us know how you would like to pay for your credits. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover as well as PayPal or similar online means. Please select the payment method that you wish to use by clicking on the icon at the top of the screen and then submitting all required information to purchase the credits. If you are paying by credit card please enter the number in full, without spaces or dashes.

  5. Submit your order

    Check the accuracy of the payment information that you have provided and correct any mistakes. When you are ready, click the 'purchase' button to submit your order. Once you place your order you will see a message stating "Thank you for your order". You will receive two confirmation emails: one from Miniclip confirming that your Miniclip Players account has been credited with the credit bundle you selected and second from the third party payment provider confirming that your credit card or other payment method has been debited the relevant amount.

Now, go and have fun on Miniclip.com!
