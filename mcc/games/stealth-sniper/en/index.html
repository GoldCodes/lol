There are 3 awards in Stealth Sniper. View All Awards

Your mission: to eliminate criminals. Your weapon: a sniper rifle. To get to the big boss you'll have to go through his henchmen. If you manage to hide a body, make a headshot or get combo kills, you'll earn bonus points. Be careful not to alert your targets! If they become too alert or manage to get away, you will fail the mission. Whatever you do, don't let the boss escape - take aim now and take them down!
